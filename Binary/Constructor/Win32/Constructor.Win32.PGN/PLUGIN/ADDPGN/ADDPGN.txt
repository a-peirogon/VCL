
 ADDPGN
 ~~~~~~

 - call when new plugins are received from internet, addpgn_attach()
 - analyze incoming buffer
 - search for RSA-key(s) via EV_ADDPGN_GETKEY event, verify RSA-signature(s)
 - attach plugins via LDRWIN32.ldrwin32_attach()

 - ALL signatures must match
 - at least TWO different signatures are required to verify incoming package
